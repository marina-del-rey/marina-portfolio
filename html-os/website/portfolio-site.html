My name is Marina Varisco Novaes, and I am a web developer based in Portugal. From a young age, I've been passionate about computing, which led me to explore different areas such as machine learning, game development and IoT. These experiences have given me a broad perspective on the many possibilites technology can offer.

I eventually discovered my passion for web development because it perfectly combines creativity and computing. I enjoy crafting websites that are both functional and engaging, as they play a critical role in helping users connect with businesses and projects. I’ve worked with a variety of technologies, including React, Django, Node.js, Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap, Spring Boot, Express.js, SCSS, MongoDB, MySQL, Docker, and Vite, amongst others. These tools have allowed me to explore different approaches to web development.

I recently earned my bachelor's degree in Informatics Engineering from the Faculty of Sciences of the University of Lisbon, where I built a strong foundation in areas like algorithms and data structures, databases, human-computer interaction, distributed systems and artifical intelligence. My coursework also included projects in software engineering, computer networks, security, and computer graphics.

Outside of coding, I enjoy singing, writing poetry, taking analog photos, drawing cats, and reading. I'm also passionate about personal growth and constantly look for ways to learn and improve. I’m excited to bring my skills and creativity to new and exciting projects!

    My Diary

    [mydiary.online]

  • A diary app that allows users to create, manage, and explore multiple diaries with detailed entries. Users can log in to create diaries, add entries and view shared diaries from other users. The application provides an intuitive interface for easy navigation and discovery, allowing users to seamlessly create, browse, and share diary entries in a connected community.

  • I designed, developed, and configured the entire application, setting up the API and managing the MongoDB database. I also configured the SMTP server for password recovery and email verification, handled deployment, and oversaw server and database administration.

    • Made with: React, Express, Node.js, Mongodb, Nginx, Docker, Postfix, Dovecot
